My Three Favorite Restaurants

In this article I will talk about three of my favorite restaurants. I don't go to restaurants often but these three are the most memorable and have pretty good food.

Three Favorite Restaurants:
  1. Canton Restaurant
  2. Majesty Chinese Restaurant
  3. Shirley's

Canton Restaurant is a great cantonese restaurant that offers both buffet and dine in. It also has a variety of cantonese food with good customer service.

What I like about this restaurant:
  • Good price
  • Good food
  • Taste of home

Majesty Chinese Restaurant is once again a chinese restaurant that is similar to the previous restaurant but a bit more costly and fancy. They have good customer service and a great variety of food. It is also very lively.

What I like about this restaurant:
  • Display of food
  • Variety

Shirley's is actually a restaurant created by someone with this name. A filipino restaurant with mostly good food and good customer service.

What I like about this restaurant:
  • Roasted pork leg
  • Easy and fast customer service from a ring of a bell
